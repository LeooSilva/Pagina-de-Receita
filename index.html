Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your chaice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filings chese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a nan-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter ar oil
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add filings (optinonal): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still sligtly runny in the middle, spinkle yout filings over ane half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filings. Let it cook for anather minute, then slide anta a plate
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional sal and pepper if needed.
